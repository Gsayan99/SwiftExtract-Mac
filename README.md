# My Extractor App
A simple, beautiful Mac app to extract .rar and .7z files. 

## How to use
1. Download the app from the Releases section.
2. You need to click "open anyway" from the Settings > Privacy & Security, as this is not downloaded from App Store.
3. After installing, if you open the app, you will see a logo in the Menu Bar, click it to expand and drag a file onto the window.
4. You do not need to open the app also, if you double click the .rar or .7z, it will extract.
5. Your files appear in the same folder!
